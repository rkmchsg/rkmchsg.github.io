About Our Classes

Our weekly children's classes follow a structured approach, gradually guiding young minds through the core principles of our cultural heritage. Each session is designed to introduce children to various value structures, imparting essential lessons in living wisely and ethically. These interactive classes encourage children to share their own experiences, creating a vibrant space where they can learn not only from their instructors but also from one another. By reflecting on their thoughts and perspectives, they gain a deeper understanding of the values that shape our world, cultivating a sense of respect, empathy, and personal growth.
