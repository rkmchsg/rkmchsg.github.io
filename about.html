About Us

These classes were founded in 1981 by the then President, Swami Sastranandaji, alongside Swami Rajeswaranandaji. The initiative began with the support of two dedicated volunteer teachers, Mrs. Usha Radhakrishnan and Mrs. Nivedita Kalavar, who played a key role in nurturing the early students. The classes started with a small group of children from the local community, mostly the children of devoted families, who were eager to learn about our cultural heritage and values.
What began as a humble effort soon blossomed into a vibrant program, with the teachers and swamis working together to instill essential life principles, cultural wisdom, and spiritual values in the next generation. The vision of Swami Sastranandaji and Swami Rajeswaranandaji, combined with the dedication of the volunteer teachers, laid the foundation for what has grown into an enduring tradition of learning, enriching countless young lives over the years.
